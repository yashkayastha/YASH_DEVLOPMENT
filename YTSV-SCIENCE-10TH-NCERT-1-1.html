Question 1
Why should a magnesium ribbon be cleaned before burning in air ?
Answer:
Magnesium gets covered with a layer of magnesium oxide when kept in air for a long time.
This layer hinders the burning of magnesium.
Hence, it is to be cleaned before burning.
